Dark

Country: Germany

Year of Release:Year of Release: 2017

Director: Baran bo Odar

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery, Drama

Synopsis: Dark is a German sci-fi thriller set in the town of Winden, where the disappearance of a boy uncovers a time travel conspiracy spanning multiple generations. Four families become entangled in mysterious events, revealing hidden secrets and the consequences of manipulating time. With its dark atmosphere and complex narrative, Dark explores fate, free will, and the cyclical nature of time.
